Our Services

Please see our services below:

Commissary Kitchen

We’re proud to offer a fully licensed, shared-use commissary kitchen designed to support local food entrepreneurs. Our kitchen provides an affordable, well-equipped space where you can prepare, cook, and store food safely and legally for sale. Whether you run a food truck, cater events, sell at markets, or have another food business, our commissary kitchen is here to help you serve the community with confidence.

Greenhouses

Our greenhouses provide a controlled, productive environment for growing fresh, healthy produce year-round. We’re proud to support local growers by offering space to cultivate vegetables, herbs, flowers, and more, no matter the season. Plus, you’ll have the opportunity to sell your freshly grown products right in our storefront, helping you reach customers who value local, high-quality goods.

Food Truck

Our food truck brings the farm to you with a delicious menu featuring our signature ice cream and gourmet hot dogs and brats. Perfect for festivals, community gatherings, or private events, our food truck is available for hire to make your occasion extra special. We’re proud to serve fresh, locally inspired flavors wherever you need us. Let us help you create a memorable, crowd-pleasing experience!
